My name is Matthew Schlake. I'm a web developer based in Carmel, Indiana.

I have always loved the feeling of building and creating things. Web development allows me to do it with my fingers!

About Me

Prior to web development, I worked in non-profit youth sport event management for almost 15 years. I found coding by volunteering to help out with our website.

To start I learned basic HTML and CSS, through online videos and tutorials. The further down the rabbit hole I went, the more I wanted to learn and code! This led me to pursue a more structured curriculum in Web Development.

When I'm not coding, I love spending time outside with my four dogs: Wren, Beau, Link, and Nova. They spend weekdays chasing each other, playing, and creating chaos; anxiously awaiting the weekend to go hiking or exploring new places!
